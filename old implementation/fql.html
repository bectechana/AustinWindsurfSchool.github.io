Austin Windsurf School - FAQ

Can I rent equipment without taking a lesson?
Yes! We offer gear rentals for experienced windsurfers who just want to hit the water on their own. Our rental rates are competitive and our equipment is top-quality. Contact us to make a reservation.
Which lesson is right for me?
If you're a total beginner or haven't windsurfed in a long time, the Spring Beginner Classes are recommended. If you are an experienced windsurfer, private lessons are more suitable.
Do you rent wetsuits?
Wetsuits can be rented from Dive World and Scubaland Adventures in Austin. We do not rent wetsuits.
Do you offer lessons for events?
Yes, contact us to learn more about our event lesson options and rates.
