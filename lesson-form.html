What To Expect - Classes & Rentals

Level 1 - Beginner Classes

$100-$189

This Spring we will be offering 6-person beginner classes. All ages, good for kids and adults, and no experienced required! Our 2-day beginner group classes cover the following:

  • 1. Wind Clock
  • 2. Rigging the equipment
  • 3. Sailing in a straight line
  • 4. Sailing upwind/downwind
  • 5. Tacking turning upwind
  • 6. Gybing turning downwind

Kids Class (13 years old & under): Please call/email for availability if your child is 13 or under!

Note: Water and air have been warm enough to go without a wetsuit. Please provide your own helmet (bike/climbing/whitewater helmets work) and wetsuit booties are recommended (not water shoes/socks).

Level 2 - Intermediate

$100-$180

Level 2 Beginner Group Classes are the perfect complement to those who took the Level 1 class or who are looking to take their windsurfing to the next level! Our 2-day beginner group classes cover the following:

  • 1. Harnessing
  • 2. Fast Tack
  • 3. Pivot Gybes
  • 4. Downwind Sailing Exercise

Depending on class skill this list may vary.

These skills are necessary for advancing with windsurfing and unlocking higher winds!

Private Lessons and Rentals

$30-$200

  • 1:1 Windsurf Lesson (2.5 hours) $200
  • 1:1 Beginner Windskate Lesson (2 hours) $100
  • 1:1 Windsurf Board and Sail Rental $30 per day

This includes a board, one sail, mast, boom and necessary accessories. Renter must have transportation for equipment. Gear pickup near Waters Park, Austin neighborhood. Not included: Helmet or wetsuit.
