About Us

Welcome to Austin Windsurf School! We offer a comprehensive range of services including group and private lessons, special classes for kids, and equipment rentals. Whether you’re a local or a visitor, an individual or a family, a beginner or an experienced windsurfer, we have everything you need to embark on an exciting windsurfing adventure.

  • Our Lessons

    • Our lessons are structured to cater to different skill levels and are designed to be as engaging and informative as possible.

  • Begginer Lesson

    • Day 1 - Dive into the world of windsurfing with our beginner lessons. You’ll learn about the wind clock, how to sail upwind, execute turns, tacks, and even some gybes. Day 2 - Building on the skills from Day 1, you’ll learn how to sail downwind, execute gybes and pivot gybes.

  • Intermediate Lesson

    • Ready to take your windsurfing skills to the next level? Our intermediate lessons cover fast tacks, pivot gybe exercises, harnessing, and beach starts.

    So, are you ready to GO WINDSURF with Austin Windsurf School? We can’t wait to welcome you!

What To Expect - Lessons & Rentals

Level 1 - Beginner Classes

$100-$189

This Spring we will be offering 6-person beginner classes. All ages, good for kids and adults, and no experienced required! Our 2-day beginner group classes cover the following:

  • 1. Wind Clock
  • 2. Rigging the equipment
  • 3. Sailing in a straight line
  • 4. Sailing upwind/downwind
  • 5. Tacking turning upwind
  • 6. Gybing turning downwind

Kids Class (13 years old & under): Please call/email for availability if your child is 13 or under!

Note: Water and air have been warm enough to go without a wetsuit. Please provide your own helmet (bike/climbing/whitewater helmets work) and wetsuit booties are recommended (not water shoes/socks).

Level 2 - Intermediate

$100-$180

Level 2 Beginner Group Classes are the perfect complement to those who took the Level 1 class or who are looking to take their windsurfing to the next level! Our 2-day beginner group classes cover the following:

  • 1. Harnessing
  • 2. Fast Tack
  • 3. Pivot Gybes
  • 4. Downwind Sailing Exercise

Depending on class skill this list may vary.

These skills are necessary for advancing with windsurfing and unlocking higher winds!

Private Lessons and Rentals

$30-$200

  • 1:1 Windsurf Lesson (2.5 hours) $200
  • 1:1 Beginner Windskate Lesson (2 hours) $100
  • 1:1 Windsurf Board and Sail Rental $30 per day

This includes a board, one sail, mast, boom and necessary accessories. Renter must have transportation for equipment. Gear pickup near Waters Park, Austin neighborhood. Not included: Helmet or wetsuit.

Lesson Cancellation Policy

If you are unable to attend a program, it is imperative to notify us as soon as possible. Refunds will be subject to the following conditions, with cancellations processed exclusively Monday to Friday:

  • More than three weeks before the start of camp/class: 75% refund.
  • Between one and three weeks before the start of camp/class: 25% refund.
  • AWS is unable to offer refunds for cancellations submitted less than one week (five business days) before the start of class.

AWS reserves the right to cancel classes without a minimum number of participants. For canceled classes, we will make every attempt to place participants in an alternate class. If unable to do so, a 100% refund will be issued.
